Care Network

Setting up and managing your family and caregiver connections

How do I set up my care network?

+

Setting up your care network is simple through the Idiom app. You can invite family members, caregivers, and healthcare providers by email. For each person, you'll specify their relationship to you and what level of insights they should receive - from basic daily summaries to detailed health pattern analysis. You can modify these permissions at any time.

Can my care network members hear my actual conversations?

+

No, care network members never receive raw audio recordings unless you explicitly choose to share specific transcript excerpts. They receive AI-generated insights about your wellbeing, social patterns, and health mentions, but not the actual conversations. You maintain complete control over what information each person receives.

What kind of insights do care network members receive?

+

Insights vary by permission level but may include daily activity summaries, mood patterns, social interaction levels, mentions of health concerns, medication discussions, and changes in routine. Family members might receive general wellness updates, while healthcare providers could access detailed health pattern analysis relevant to your care.

Can I remove someone from my care network?

+

Yes, you can instantly remove any member from your care network or modify their access permissions at any time. When you remove someone, they immediately lose access to all insights and future updates. You can also choose to delete any historical data they previously had access to.
