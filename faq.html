Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How is this different from just recording everything?

+

Traditional recording devices simply capture audio. Idiom goes beyond recording by using advanced AI to understand the context and meaning of conversations. We automatically generate insights about emotional wellbeing, health patterns, social connections, and potential concerns - all while maintaining strict privacy controls. Your care network receives meaningful summaries and alerts, not raw recordings.

How does the device work?

+

The Idiom device is a lightweight, wearable monitor that continuously captures conversations and environmental sounds. It uses advanced speech recognition to create transcripts, then applies AI analysis to generate care insights. The device syncs with our secure cloud platform to share appropriate information with your designated care network. Raw audio is processed and deleted within 24 hours for privacy protection.

Do I need a smartphone to use Idiom?

+

While a smartphone enhances the Idiom experience with additional features like GPS context and emergency response, it's not required for basic operation. The device has built-in connectivity and can function independently. However, we recommend using the companion app for optimal setup, privacy controls, and care network management.

How long does the battery last?

+

The Idiom device provides 24-48 hours of continuous monitoring on a single charge, depending on usage patterns. The device includes smart power management and will alert you and your care network when charging is needed. We also provide a portable charging dock for easy charging throughout the day.

What happens if I lose my Idiom device?

+

Lost devices can be remotely located through GPS tracking (if enabled) and can be remotely wiped to protect your privacy. We provide device replacement as part of our service, and all your settings and care network configurations are safely stored in the cloud. You can quickly restore everything to a new device without losing any care insights or historical data.

Privacy & Consent

How is this different from surveillance?

+

Surveillance implies secretive monitoring without consent. Idiom is built on transparency and explicit consent. You control who sees what information, when they see it, and for how long. Every person in your care network is there because you specifically invited them. You can revoke access instantly, and all insights are designed to support your wellbeing, not monitor your behavior for external purposes.

Do I need consent from other people in my conversations?

+

In most jurisdictions, you can record conversations you participate in without additional consent. However, we recommend informing frequent visitors about Idiom as a courtesy. The device can be configured with privacy zones and schedules to respect others' privacy preferences while maintaining your care monitoring needs.

Can I control what my family members see?

+

Absolutely. You have granular control over what each care network member receives. You can set different family members to receive only basic wellness summaries, while giving healthcare providers access to detailed health pattern analysis. These permissions can be changed at any time, and you always retain full control over your data.

What about private conversations with my spouse or therapist?

+

You can configure privacy zones, specific times, or trigger words that automatically exclude certain conversations from processing or sharing. Additionally, you can designate specific relationships (like spouse or therapist) where conversations are kept completely private or shared only with medical professionals if relevant to your care.

Can I turn off recording completely?

+

Yes, you can pause recording at any time using the device button, mobile app, or voice command. You can also set automatic schedules for when recording should be paused (like during certain hours or activities) and create permanent privacy zones where recording never occurs.

Data Security

How secure is my data?

+

Your data is protected with enterprise-grade security including end-to-end AES-256 encryption, zero-knowledge architecture, and secure data centers in Australia. We use multi-factor authentication, role-based access controls, and undergo regular security audits. All audio is processed immediately and deleted within 24 hours unless you choose to retain it.

How long do you keep my recordings?

+

Raw audio is automatically deleted within 24 hours after processing. Transcripts and insights are retained according to your preferences, with options from 1 day to 7 years, or permanent retention with explicit consent. You have complete control over retention periods and can delete any data instantly at any time.

Can I export or delete my data?

+

Yes, you have complete control over your data. You can download a complete copy of all personal data in portable format, delete specific conversations or time periods, or permanently delete your entire account. All deletions are immediate and include removal from backup systems within 90 days.

Where is my data stored?

+

Your data is primarily stored in secure facilities in Australia to minimize cross-border transfers. We use trusted cloud providers including AWS Australia and Google Cloud Asia-Pacific with strict contractual protections. Some subprocessors may be located outside Australia, but we ensure adequate protection through standard contractual clauses.

Do you sell my data or use it for advertising?

+

Never. We will never sell your personal data or use it for advertising. Our business model is built entirely on subscription services, ensuring our incentives align with protecting your privacy. We may create aggregate, de-identified data for improving care algorithms, but this never contains personal information and cannot be traced back to you.

Care Network

How do I set up my care network?

+

Setting up your care network is simple through the Idiom app. You can invite family members, caregivers, and healthcare providers by email. For each person, you'll specify their relationship to you and what level of insights they should receive - from basic daily summaries to detailed health pattern analysis. You can modify these permissions at any time.

Can my care network members hear my actual conversations?

+

No, care network members never receive raw audio recordings unless you explicitly choose to share specific transcript excerpts. They receive AI-generated insights about your wellbeing, social patterns, and health mentions, but not the actual conversations. You maintain complete control over what information each person receives.

What kind of insights do care network members receive?

+

Insights vary by permission level but may include daily activity summaries, mood patterns, social interaction levels, mentions of health concerns, medication discussions, and changes in routine. Family members might receive general wellness updates, while healthcare providers could access detailed health pattern analysis relevant to your care.

Can I remove someone from my care network?

+

Yes, you can instantly remove any member from your care network or modify their access permissions at any time. When you remove someone, they immediately lose access to all insights and future updates. You can also choose to delete any historical data they previously had access to.

Health & Medical

Is Idiom HIPAA compliant?

+

We are working toward HIPAA compliance for healthcare integrations and will be fully compliant before launch. Idiom currently meets Australian Privacy Act requirements and health records legislation. When sharing data with healthcare providers, we ensure all transmissions meet medical privacy standards and require explicit patient consent.

Can my doctor access my Idiom data?

+

Only with your explicit written consent. You can authorize specific healthcare providers to receive relevant health insights, medication mentions, and patterns that could inform your care. Doctors never receive raw conversations, only medically relevant summaries and alerts that you've specifically approved for sharing.

Does Idiom work with other health apps or devices?

+

Yes, Idiom can integrate with health apps, fitness trackers, medical alert systems, and electronic health records with your permission. These integrations provide additional context for care insights and can enhance emergency response capabilities. All integrations require explicit consent and follow strict privacy protocols.

Can Idiom detect medical emergencies?

+

Idiom can detect potential emergencies through conversation analysis, environmental monitoring, and integration with medical alert systems. It can identify distress calls, falls, unusual patterns, or specific trigger words you configure. Emergency contacts and services can be automatically notified based on your settings and the severity of the detected situation.

Pricing & Support

Is Idiom free?

+

Idiom includes a service period with your device purchase, after which a subscription is required for continued monitoring and insights. We offer flexible pricing plans based on your care needs, from basic family monitoring to comprehensive healthcare integration. Pricing details will be available at launch.

What happens after the included service period?

+

After the included service period, you'll need a subscription to continue receiving care insights and cloud features. The device will continue basic recording functionality, but cloud processing, care network sharing, and AI insights require an active subscription. You can choose from several plans based on your needs.

Is Idiom compatible with hearing aids or other medical devices?

+

Idiom is designed to work alongside hearing aids, medical alert systems, and other assistive technologies. The device uses safe frequencies that don't interfere with medical equipment. We can also integrate with many medical devices to provide comprehensive care monitoring when authorized by your healthcare provider.

What languages does Idiom support?

+

Idiom currently supports English with high accuracy. We're actively developing support for additional languages based on user needs. Multi-language households can be configured to recognize and process different languages within the same environment, ensuring comprehensive monitoring for diverse families.

How accurate is the transcription?

+

Idiom uses advanced speech recognition technology with high accuracy rates, typically over 95% in clear conditions. Accuracy improves over time as the system learns your voice patterns and environment. The focus is on capturing meaningful insights rather than perfect transcription, so care analysis remains effective even with minor transcription variations.

Can I use Idiom offline?

+

Idiom can record locally when offline, but requires internet connectivity for AI processing and sharing insights with your care network. Recorded content is automatically processed when connectivity is restored. Emergency features may work in limited offline mode depending on your configuration and local emergency services integration.
